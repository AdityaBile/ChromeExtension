                                  how this works / find out more / help

Epic's encrypted proxy hides your IP address and location. You will appear to be in the country you select. Encryption and IP masking protects your browsing from hackers, trackers, your ISP and the government.

Since you will appear to be in a new location, sites such as Facebook, Gmail and others may require additional authentication. Don't worry that's actually a good sign and more secure.

Your browsing speeds may be slightly slower. In case browsing isn't working at all or is very slow, a particular server may be down. Please try a different country or close and re-open Epic to connect to a different node.

Unfortunately many sites (primarily to stop bots and data scraping) block access via VPN or encrypted proxy. If a site isn't loading or you get an error, you can now whitelist it by disabling the encrypted proxy via the proxy or umbrella buttons for just that site. After whitelisting a site, refresh the page to try to load it.
